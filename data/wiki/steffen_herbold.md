# Steffen Herbold

**Type:** author

Equal contributing author from the Faculty of Computer Science and Mathematics at the University of Passau.

---

Steffen Herbold is a computer scientist and equal contributing author of the paper introducing the formal uncertainty framework for LLM text generation, affiliated with the Faculty of Computer Science and Mathematics at the University of Passau, Germany. His research interests span empirical software engineering, machine learning, and the analysis of AI-generated content, with a particular focus on the reliability and quality of automated systems. In the paper, Herbold co-developed the formal definitions of the sampling tree, filter functions, objective functions, and the associated uncertainty measures. His contributions help bridge foundational formal methods with practical concerns in the deployment of large language models.
