# Haoyu Wang

**Type:** author

PhD student at Singapore Management University and primary author of ProbGuard.

---

Haoyu Wang is a PhD student at Singapore Management University and the primary author of the ProbGuard paper, which introduces a proactive probabilistic runtime monitoring framework for LLM agent safety. His research focuses on the intersection of formal methods, probabilistic modeling, and the safe deployment of autonomous AI agents. Wang's work on ProbGuard contributes to the growing body of research on runtime verification and safety assurance for large language model-based systems. The ProbGuard framework represents a significant contribution to the field of AI safety, combining techniques from formal verification, statistical learning theory, and autonomous agent research.
