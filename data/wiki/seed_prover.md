# Seed-Prover

**Type:** method

A theorem proving method using deep and broad reasoning that represents a state-of-the-art approach on the miniF2F benchmark.

---

Seed-Prover is a theorem proving method that employs deep and broad reasoning strategies to achieve state-of-the-art performance on the miniF2F formal mathematics benchmark, combining extensive proof search with sophisticated reasoning over mathematical structures. It represents one of the most capable prior approaches on miniF2F, demonstrating the progress that LLM-driven methods have achieved on olympiad-level formal mathematics. Like other miniF2F-focused methods, Seed-Prover's design and evaluation are oriented toward relatively self-contained mathematical problems rather than the large, interdependent proof corpora characteristic of industrial verification projects. The AutoReal paper situates its contributions against methods like Seed-Prover to motivate the need for approaches specifically designed for industrial-scale settings such as seL4.
