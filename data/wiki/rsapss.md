# RSAPSS

**Type:** dataset

An AFP project formalizing the RSA-PSS signature scheme in Isabelle/HOL, containing 278 theorems used to evaluate AutoReal-Prover.

---

RSAPSS is an Archive of Formal Proofs project that provides a formal Isabelle/HOL development of the RSA Probabilistic Signature Scheme (RSA-PSS), a standardized digital signature algorithm. The project contains 278 theorems and constitutes the largest of the three AFP evaluation benchmarks used in the AutoReal paper to assess proof generalization. AutoReal-Prover is evaluated on these theorems without additional domain-specific fine-tuning, testing its ability to handle formal reasoning about number-theoretic and cryptographic constructions. The project's scale and mathematical content make it a representative test of the model's capacity for industrial-strength formal verification beyond its seL4 training distribution.
