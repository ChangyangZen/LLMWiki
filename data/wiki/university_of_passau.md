# University of Passau

**Type:** institution

German research institution where both authors are affiliated, within the Faculty of Computer Science and Mathematics.

---

The University of Passau is a public research university located in Passau, Bavaria, Germany, known for its strengths in computer science, law, economics, and the humanities. Within its Faculty of Computer Science and Mathematics, researchers pursue work in areas including software engineering, data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. The university is the institutional home of Steffen Herbold and Florian Lemmerich, the authors of the formal uncertainty framework for LLM text generation. As a mid-sized German university with an international research profile, Passau contributes to European efforts in foundational and applied AI research.
