# Vikash Singh

**Type:** author

First author of the VERGE paper, affiliated with Case Western Reserve University.

---

Vikash Singh is the first author of the paper introducing the VERGE framework, affiliated with Case Western Reserve University. His research contributions in the VERGE work span the design and implementation of the neurosymbolic pipeline, including the iterative refinement loop, semantic routing mechanism, and integration of SMT-based verification. Singh's background bridges natural language processing and formal methods, reflecting the interdisciplinary nature of the VERGE project. The paper represents a collaboration between his academic institution and researchers at Amazon Web Services.
