# Singapore Management University

**Type:** institution

Primary institution affiliated with the ProbGuard research, home to three of the four authors.

---

Singapore Management University (SMU) is a research university located in Singapore known for its strengths in information systems, computing, and business disciplines. Three of the four authors of the ProbGuard paper are affiliated with SMU, making it the primary institutional home of the ProbGuard research project. SMU's School of Computing and Information Systems hosts research groups working on formal methods, AI safety, and software engineering, providing the academic environment in which ProbGuard was developed. The university's emphasis on applied and interdisciplinary research aligns with ProbGuard's integration of probabilistic model checking, machine learning, and autonomous agent safety.
