# Amazon Web Services

**Type:** institution

Research institution where the majority of VERGE authors are affiliated and where the work was primarily conducted.

---

Amazon Web Services (AWS) is the cloud computing subsidiary of Amazon.com and a major research institution in the fields of machine learning, natural language processing, and artificial intelligence. The majority of the authors of the VERGE paper are affiliated with AWS, where the primary research and development of the framework was conducted. AWS has a broad portfolio of AI research spanning foundation models, automated reasoning, and applied machine learning, situating VERGE within a broader organizational interest in reliable and verifiable AI systems. The collaboration between AWS researchers and academic partners at Case Western Reserve University reflects a common model of industry-academic partnership in contemporary AI research.
