# ProofAug

**Type:** method

An efficient neural theorem proving method via fine-grained proof structure analysis that has improved performance on mathematics-oriented benchmarks.

---

ProofAug is an efficient neural theorem proving method that improves proof generation through fine-grained analysis of proof structure, identifying and exploiting the compositional organization of formal proofs to guide search. It has demonstrated improved performance on mathematics-oriented formal benchmarks, contributing to progress on miniF2F and related evaluation suites. ProofAug's approach of leveraging structural information within proofs complements training-based methods by providing more targeted guidance during proof search. In the AutoReal paper, ProofAug is referenced as part of the landscape of prior neural theorem proving methods whose focus on olympiad-level mathematics benchmarks contrasts with AutoReal's orientation toward industrial-scale verification.
