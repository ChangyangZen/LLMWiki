# Florian Lemmerich

**Type:** author

Equal contributing author from the Faculty of Computer Science and Mathematics at the University of Passau.

---

Florian Lemmerich is a computer scientist and equal contributing author of the paper on formal uncertainty analysis of LLM text generation, based at the Faculty of Computer Science and Mathematics at the University of Passau, Germany. His research encompasses machine learning, data mining, and the interpretability and evaluation of AI systems. In the paper, Lemmerich co-designed the formal framework, contributing to the mathematical formalization of the sampling tree structure, objective-weighted probability measures, and the unification of existing uncertainty quantification methods within the framework. His work emphasizes the importance of rigorous formal foundations for understanding and improving the reliability of large language models.
