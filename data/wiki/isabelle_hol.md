# Isabelle/HOL

**Type:** method

An interactive theorem prover and proof assistant for higher-order logic, used as the formal verification framework for seL4 and AFP projects.

---

Isabelle/HOL is an interactive theorem prover and proof assistant for higher-order logic, developed jointly at the University of Cambridge and Technische Universität München. It provides a rich tactic language for constructing formal proofs and supports large-scale verification projects through modular theory development and a structured proof language called Isar. Isabelle/HOL serves as the formal verification framework for both the seL4 microkernel project and the Archive of Formal Proofs, making it a central platform for industrial and academic formal verification. In the context of AutoReal, Isabelle/HOL acts as the external proof checker that validates whether model-generated proof scripts are formally correct.
