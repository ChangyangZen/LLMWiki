# Healthcare Workflow Automation

**Type:** concept

High-stakes enterprise workflows including prior authorization, clinical document processing, and billing automation requiring determinism, auditability, and HIPAA/CMS compliance.

---

Healthcare Workflow Automation refers to the application of compiled AI to high-stakes administrative and clinical processes in the healthcare industry, including prior authorization, clinical document processing, and medical billing automation. These workflows impose stringent requirements for determinism, auditability, and regulatory compliance under frameworks such as HIPAA and CMS guidelines, making the unpredictability of runtime inference approaches particularly problematic. Compiled AI's guarantee of reproducible, inspectable execution traces aligns directly with audit trail requirements imposed by healthcare regulators, allowing compliance officers to review exact decision logic rather than probabilistic model outputs. The paper presents healthcare automation as a primary motivating use case and demonstrates the paradigm's applicability through a detailed prior authorization workflow example.
