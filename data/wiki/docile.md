# DocILE

**Type:** dataset

A document intelligence benchmark of 5,680 invoice documents with degraded OCR quality, heterogeneous formats, and semantic ambiguity, evaluated using KILE and LIR metrics.

---

DocILE is a document intelligence benchmark consisting of 5,680 invoice documents characterized by degraded OCR quality, heterogeneous layouts, and significant semantic ambiguity across document types. The benchmark is evaluated using two complementary metrics, KILE for key field extraction and LIR for structured line item recognition, making it a rigorous test of both entity-level and relational extraction capabilities. Its realistic noise and format diversity make DocILE substantially more challenging than clean synthetic benchmarks, reflecting conditions encountered in enterprise document processing pipelines. In the compiled AI paper, DocILE serves as the primary evaluation surface for document intelligence tasks, where the Code Factory variant is assessed against runtime inference baselines.
