# Qwen3.5-9B

**Type:** model

A 9B parameter LLM used in MiCP experiments, showing strong performance in early-stopping efficiency especially on 2WikiMHQA.

---

Qwen3.5-9B is a nine billion parameter large language model developed by Alibaba and used as one of three backbone models in the MiCP experimental evaluation. The model demonstrates strong performance in early stopping efficiency within the MiCP framework, particularly on the 2WikiMultiHopQA benchmark, where its uncertainty signals are well-calibrated for conformal threshold learning. Its inclusion in MiCP experiments alongside open-source and proprietary alternatives helps validate that the framework's coverage and efficiency benefits are not artifacts of a particular model's characteristics. Qwen3.5-9B represents the class of competitive mid-scale open language models increasingly used in research and production reasoning pipelines.
