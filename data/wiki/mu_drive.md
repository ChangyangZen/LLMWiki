# μDrive

**Type:** dataset

A user-controlled framework for generating diverse traffic violation scenarios, used to create law-violating test cases for autonomous driving evaluation.

---

μDrive is a user-controlled framework for programmatically generating diverse traffic violation scenarios in autonomous driving simulation environments, enabling systematic testing of autonomous vehicle safety systems. By providing fine-grained control over scenario parameters such as vehicle speeds, trajectories, and traffic configurations, μDrive allows researchers to construct targeted test cases that exercise specific safety properties and traffic law violations. In ProbGuard's autonomous driving evaluation, μDrive is used to generate law-violating scenarios that stress-test the framework's ability to predict and prevent traffic safety violations before they occur. The diversity of scenarios produced by μDrive contributes to the robustness and generalizability of ProbGuard's evaluation results.
