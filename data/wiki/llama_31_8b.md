# Llama 3.1 8B

**Type:** model

An 8B parameter large language model from Meta used as a backbone for FoVer-PRMs and for generating step-by-step solutions in FoVer-40K.

---

Llama 3.1 8B is an 8-billion parameter large language model developed and released by Meta as part of the Llama 3.1 model family, known for strong performance across a range of natural language understanding and generation tasks. In the FoVer framework, Llama 3.1 8B serves a dual role: it is used to generate step-by-step solutions for formal reasoning tasks during the construction of the FoVer-40K dataset, and it is also used as the backbone model for fine-tuning FoVer-PRMs. The resulting FoVer-PRM based on Llama 3.1 8B is evaluated against comparable existing PRMs such as RLHFlow-Llama3.1-8B PRM across a suite of 12 benchmarks.
