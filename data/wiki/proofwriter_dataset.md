# ProofWriter Dataset

**Type:** dataset

A synthetic dataset for evaluating deductive reasoning and proof generation over natural language rule sets.

---

ProofWriter is a synthetic benchmark for evaluating deductive reasoning in which models must derive conclusions from a set of natural language rules and facts organized into chains of logical inference. The dataset is structured at multiple depths of reasoning, allowing evaluation of how well systems handle increasingly long chains of deduction. ProofWriter is included in the VERGE evaluation suite because its rule-based structure is well-suited to formalization and SMT-based verification, making it a natural test bed for neurosymbolic approaches. The synthetic and controlled nature of ProofWriter allows precise measurement of reasoning depth capabilities independent of world knowledge confounds.
