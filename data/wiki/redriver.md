# REDriver

**Type:** method

A runtime enforcement approach for autonomous vehicles using quantitative semantics to measure robustness degree of property satisfaction or violation.

---

REDriver is a runtime enforcement approach for autonomous vehicles that uses quantitative semantics to measure the robustness degree of property satisfaction or violation, providing a graded assessment of how close a system is to violating a safety property. Rather than producing binary pass/fail verdicts, REDriver computes a robustness score that reflects the margin by which a property is satisfied or violated, enabling more nuanced enforcement decisions. The approach is designed for autonomous driving scenarios and leverages formal traffic safety specifications to guide enforcement. REDriver is evaluated alongside ProbGuard in the autonomous driving domain, providing a comparative baseline for reactive enforcement approaches.
