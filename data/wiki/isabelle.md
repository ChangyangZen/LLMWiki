# Isabelle/HOL

**Type:** method

An interactive theorem prover applicable to higher-order logic, used in FoVer to verify formal theorem proving tasks step by step.

---

Isabelle/HOL is an interactive theorem prover based on higher-order logic, widely used in formal mathematics and computer science for verifying the correctness of mathematical proofs and programs. In the FoVer framework, Isabelle is employed to verify step-by-step formal proofs generated by LLMs for theorem proving tasks derived from GSM8K, checking whether each proof step is logically valid within the Isabelle proof environment. Because Isabelle enforces strict formal proof rules, it provides highly accurate step-level correctness judgments that serve as reliable training labels for PRMs. The integration of Isabelle into FoVer enables the automatic annotation of theorem proving solutions without requiring human mathematical expertise.
