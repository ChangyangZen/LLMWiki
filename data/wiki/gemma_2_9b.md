# Gemma-2-9B-IT

**Type:** model

An open-source instruction-tuned LLM of 9B parameters used as one of three backbone models in MiCP experiments.

---

Gemma-2-9B-IT is an open-source instruction-tuned large language model with nine billion parameters developed by Google DeepMind, designed to follow natural language instructions across a wide range of tasks. In the MiCP paper, Gemma-2-9B-IT serves as one of three backbone language models used to evaluate the framework across five question answering benchmarks, testing whether MiCP's conformal guarantees and efficiency benefits generalize across different model families. Its inclusion alongside Qwen3.5-9B and GPT-4o-mini ensures that experimental conclusions are not specific to any single model architecture or training paradigm. Results with Gemma-2-9B-IT contribute to demonstrating MiCP's model-agnostic applicability.
