# BBEH Dataset

**Type:** dataset

Big-Bench Extra Hard, a challenging benchmark for evaluating complex reasoning capabilities of language models.

---

Big-Bench Extra Hard (BBEH) is a highly challenging reasoning benchmark derived from the BIG-Bench suite, designed to contain tasks that remain difficult even for state-of-the-art large language models after standard prompting and fine-tuning. The benchmark spans a diverse range of reasoning categories including mathematical, logical, and algorithmic tasks, providing a broad assessment of advanced reasoning capabilities. VERGE includes BBEH in its evaluation suite to demonstrate performance gains on tasks that push the boundaries of current LLM reasoning, beyond the more structured logical inference benchmarks. Results on BBEH reflect the generalization of the VERGE framework to heterogeneous and unpredictable reasoning challenges.
