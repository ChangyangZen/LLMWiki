# HLE Dataset

**Type:** dataset

Humanity's Last Exam, an extremely challenging benchmark testing advanced reasoning across diverse academic domains.

---

Humanity's Last Exam (HLE) is an extremely challenging benchmark designed to test advanced reasoning across a wide variety of academic and professional domains, with problems sourced from expert-level examinations and competitions in mathematics, science, law, and other fields. HLE is intended to probe the upper limits of LLM reasoning capability, with problems that are difficult even for highly capable frontier models. Its inclusion in the VERGE evaluation suite serves to assess whether formal verification and iterative refinement provide measurable benefits at the frontier of model capability, where single-pass approaches already perform at high levels. Performance on HLE is a stringent test of whether the VERGE framework offers systematic gains beyond marginal cases.
