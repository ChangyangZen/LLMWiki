# Apollo Autonomous Driving Platform

**Type:** model

An open-source autonomous driving simulator integrated with ProbGuard to monitor and enforce traffic safety properties in driving scenarios.

---

The Apollo Autonomous Driving Platform is an open-source autonomous driving framework developed by Baidu, providing a comprehensive simulation and software stack for developing, testing, and evaluating autonomous vehicle systems. In the ProbGuard evaluation, Apollo serves as the simulation environment for autonomous driving experiments, generating execution traces of vehicle behavior in diverse traffic scenarios. ProbGuard integrates with Apollo to monitor vehicle state information in real time, applying its probabilistic risk prediction framework to enforce compliance with formal traffic safety properties. Apollo's realistic simulation capabilities make it a suitable testbed for evaluating proactive safety monitoring in safety-critical autonomous vehicle settings.
