# Logic-LM

**Type:** model

A neurosymbolic baseline that empowers LLMs with symbolic solvers for logical reasoning, used as a comparison system.

---

Logic-LM is a neurosymbolic framework that augments large language models with symbolic logic solvers to improve performance on structured logical reasoning tasks, translating natural language problems into formal logical representations that are solved by an external prover. The system represents an early and influential approach to combining the language understanding capabilities of LLMs with the deductive precision of symbolic AI. In the VERGE evaluation, Logic-LM serves as a comparison baseline to situate VERGE within the broader landscape of neurosymbolic reasoning systems. VERGE extends beyond Logic-LM's approach by introducing iterative refinement, semantic routing, and MCS-based error localization rather than relying on a single-pass translation and solve paradigm.
