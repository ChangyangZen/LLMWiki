# Qwen 2.5 7B

**Type:** model

A 7B parameter large language model from the Qwen team used as a backbone for FoVer-PRMs and for generating step-by-step solutions in FoVer-40K.

---

Qwen 2.5 7B is a 7-billion parameter large language model developed by the Qwen team, recognized for competitive performance on mathematical and logical reasoning tasks relative to similarly sized models. Within the FoVer framework, Qwen 2.5 7B is used both to generate step-by-step solutions for formal reasoning tasks during FoVer-40K construction and as a backbone for fine-tuning FoVer-PRMs. The FoVer-PRM built on Qwen 2.5 7B is benchmarked against Qwen2.5-Math-PRM-7B and other baselines across diverse reasoning tasks, demonstrating the effectiveness of formally verified training labels.
