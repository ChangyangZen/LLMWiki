# Elliptic_Curves_Group_Law

**Type:** dataset

An AFP project formalizing the group law for Weierstrass elliptic curves, containing 109 theorems used to evaluate AutoReal-Prover.

---

Elliptic_Curves_Group_Law is an Archive of Formal Proofs project that formalizes the group law for elliptic curves defined by Weierstrass equations in Isabelle/HOL, providing rigorous proofs of algebraic properties such as associativity of the elliptic curve group operation. It contains 109 theorems and is one of three AFP projects used in the AutoReal paper to evaluate the generalization of AutoReal-Prover to security-related formal mathematics. The project's algebraic geometry content presents a distinct reasoning domain compared to the systems verification proofs in seL4, providing a test of the model's breadth across formal mathematical developments. Its inclusion reflects the relevance of elliptic curve cryptography to contemporary security verification.
